Hot Tours

Spain, Benidorm

54 customer reviews

Located along the sunny shores of Costa Blanca, Benidorm is renowned as a vibrant resort town perfect for beach holidays. Its long-standing status as one of Spain's original beach destinations ensures a wealth of entertainment, from lively nightlife and bustling markets to family-friendly attractions and pristine beaches.

Benidorm's appeal as a vibrant resort town extends beyond its beaches to encompass a diverse array of cultural and recreational activities. Visitors can explore historic sites such as the Castell de Benidorm, indulge in local cuisine at seaside restaurants, or embark on outdoor adventures.
